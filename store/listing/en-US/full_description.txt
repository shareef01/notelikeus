Notelikeus is a fast, private notes app inspired by Google Keep. Your notes live on your device in an encrypted database — sign in with Google when you want optional sync across devices.

CAPTURE IDEAS YOUR WAY
• Write notes with titles, colors, and rich text (bold, italic, links)
• Build checklists and organize with labels
• Pin important notes

STAY ON TOP OF THINGS
• Search across titles, body text, checklists, and labels
• Archive and trash with undo
• Set date/time reminders with notifications
• Reorder notes in list view

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• Encrypted local database (SQLCipher)
• Optional biometric lock per note or for the whole app
• Locked notes stay off the cloud
• No ads, no analytics

CLOUD SYNC (OPTIONAL)
• Sign in with Google to sync note text via Firebase
• Auto-sync when signed in, or sync manually from Settings
• Works on Firebase free (Spark) tier — no paid plan required for text sync

BACKUP & WIDGET
• Export and import notes as JSON anytime
• Home screen widget for quick access

Notelikeus works offline. Cloud sync is optional and only runs when you sign in.
